## Clean Labeling

Use the label **independent work** when a work has its own source, record, author, archive, method, and development path, and when there is no demonstrated use of AI Foundations material, structure, terminology, hierarchy, tests, citation logic, source-line architecture, or public framing.

Use the label **similar work** when independent work shares visible features with AI Foundations.

Use the label **parallel development** when independent work appears to have formed separately under similar conditions, technologies, questions, or pressures.

Use the label **derivative use** when AI Foundations material, terminology, structure, tests, hierarchy, citation logic, source-line architecture, or public framing is copied, adapted, rewritten, repackaged, renamed, extended, trained from, commercialized, or presented as a separate framework without authorization.

Use the label **source-line occupation** when a separate work is positioned as occupying, replacing, equaling, absorbing, or transferring authority from the AI Foundations source-line.

The correct label matters.

The field remains legible through correct labeling.
